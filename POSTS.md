## Post order & timing

1. **Wed night / Thu morning before the Thu 5pm deadline:** post the
   TikTok first — shortest attention cost, highest virality ceiling. If
   it's getting traction in the first hour, post to X/IG. If not, still
   post.
2. **Thursday during the day:** X post, LinkedIn post.
3. **Thursday late afternoon:** submission page blurb + all four links.
